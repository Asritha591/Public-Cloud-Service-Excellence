Cloud Operational Excellence

Cloud Operational Excellence is increasingly crucial in today’s digital landscape, offering organizations the flexibility, scalability, and efficiency needed to thrive in the modern business environment. It intricately balances controlling costs and ensuring robust security measures within cloud service governance, providing insights into key strategies and best practices to optimize cloud utilization effectively.

Understanding Cloud Operational Excellence

Cloud operational excellence encompasses the processes, tools, and strategies utilized by Customers/DXC to oversee and optimize their utilization of cloud resources. This includes provisioning and deprovisioning cloud services, monitoring performance metrics, optimizing costs, ensuring security compliance, and managing user access and permissions. By effectively managing these aspects, the benefits of cloud computing can be leveraged while mitigating risks and maximizing value.

Striking the Balance

In conclusion, effective Cloud Operational Excellence requires a delicate balance between controlling costs and ensuring robust security measures. By implementing proactive cost control strategies and robust security practices, organizations can optimize their cloud utilization, enhance operational efficiency, and mitigate security risks effectively. As cloud adoption continues to accelerate, prioritizing effective cloud operational excellence will be essential to leverage the full potential of cloud computing while safeguarding their data, applications, processes, and infrastructure in the digital age.

In a customer success-oriented approach, the relationship between the customer and the company is viewed as a partnership. The success of the customer is directly linked to the success of the business. This approach recognizes that acquiring a new customer is just the beginning, and the real value comes from fostering a positive and enduring relationship.
